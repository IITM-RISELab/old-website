Welcome to R.I.S.E

Reconfigurable Intelligent Systems Engineering Laboratory

R.I.S.E. Group is a research group in the CSE Dept. of IIT Madras working in the areas of Computer Architecture, Security, Machine Learning and VLSI Design. If you wish to collaborate with us, please contact the relevant Faculty members from the People's page.

About us

The RISE group of laboratories was started in 2002, with an aim to focus on reconfigurable computing systems and artificial intelligence. Over the years RISE has played host to 7 faculties, over 100+ research scholars and nearly 500 undergraduate students.

The Rise group of laboratories consists of 4 labs namely the Interactive inteliigence lab, the computer architecture and system design lab, the indeginous microprocessor development (SHAKTI) lab and the secure systems group.
